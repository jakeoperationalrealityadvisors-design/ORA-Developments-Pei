🍎 iPhone / iPad Users

The easiest way to use SmartShop on iPhone is through Safari. You can even add it to your Home Screen for quick one-tap access.

  1. Tap Open SmartShop above.
  2. If needed, open the page in Safari.
  3. Tap the Share button in Safari.
  4. Scroll and tap Add to Home Screen.
  5. Tap Add.
  6. Open SmartShop anytime from your Home Screen.
If you opened the link from Facebook or another app, look for Open in Safari first. After that, adding it to your Home Screen is easy.

🤖 Android Users

You can use SmartShop right in your browser, or request the Android version through your SmartShop account if you prefer having it installed on your device.

  1. For the simplest option, tap Open SmartShop.
  2. Log in and start using it right away.
  3. If you want the installed Android version, tap Download Android APK.
  4. You may be asked to log in or confirm access before download.
  5. Open the file on your Android phone and follow the install prompts.
  6. Once installed, open SmartShop and your account should sync normally.
Not into downloading apps outside the Play Store? No worries at all — the web app is the easiest option and works great.
